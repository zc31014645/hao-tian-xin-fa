第二條 教旨 Article Two: Teachings
本教以生生不息，體天心之仁，親親仁民，仁民愛物為中心思想。旨在先盡人道，正心修身，齊家治國；再修天道，積功累德，救世度人；進而修持身心性命妙道，恢宏天人合一思想真諦，致力于溝通天人文化，窮究宇宙真理，積極向天奮鬥，揭破自然奧秘，明瞭生命來源，悟徹人生究竟，守和全形，雙修性命，把握現實軀體，勤參法華上乘，效法天體運行，向自己奮鬥，以求聖凡平等，而達天人大同之終極目標。 This religion is centered on the idea of continuous life, embodying the benevolence of the Heavenly Heart, fostering love among people, and caring for all living beings. Its aim is first to fulfill human duties, correct the heart, cultivate oneself, and govern the family and state; then to cultivate the Dao of Heaven, accumulate merit and virtue, save and guide humanity; further, to practice the profound Dao of body, mind, and life, expound the true essence of the unity of Heaven and Humanity, and strive to connect Heaven and human culture. The goal is to explore the truths of the universe, actively strive towards Heaven, uncover natural mysteries, understand the origins of life, grasp the ultimate meaning of life, maintain harmony and integrity, cultivate both body and life, understand the real physical form, diligently study the Lotus Sutra, align with the cosmic operations, strive towards oneself, seek equality between the sacred and the mundane, and achieve the ultimate goal of universal harmony between Heaven and humanity.

This translation maintains the comprehensive and nuanced nature of the original text while conveying its religious and philosophical teachings.
